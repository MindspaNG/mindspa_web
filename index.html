About us

Mind-Spa is a platform for the rejuvenation of the mind - Heal, Mend, Renew. It addresses major mental health challenges such as severe anxiety, depression, schizo-active disorders, and re-occurring symptoms associated with a number of mental health challenges prevalent in the West-African communities.

Mission statement

To become the number one provider of easily accessible mental health services in West Africa.

Vision statement

To create awareness, provide access to wellness, and chart a pathway for mental wellbeing.

Goals

We are creating an avenue to provide access to holistic health and promote mental wellbeing.

What we offer

Learn about mental illness, including signs and symptoms.

Receive supportive counselling about daily life and strategies for stress management.

Be monitored closely for conditions requiring more intensive care.
